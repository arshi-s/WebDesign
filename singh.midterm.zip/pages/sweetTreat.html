Best For A Sweet Treat

Indulge in Rome’s delectable desserts!

Pasticceria Regoli

Pasticceria Regoli, a century-old bakery in the heart of Rome, is a haven for sweet-toothed locals. Known for its fresh pastries and classic Italian desserts, this family-run pasticceria has been a beloved institution since 1916. Their iconic maritozzo—a soft brioche bun filled with whipped cream—is a must-try. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso and pastry or taking a box of treats to go, Regoli offers a true taste of Roman sweetness.

Gelateria del Teatro

Gelateria del Teatro is a hidden gem located on a quaint alley near Piazza Navona, offering some of the best artisanal gelato in Rome. This gelateria is known for its innovative flavors made with fresh, natural ingredients, like Sicilian pistachios, Amalfi lemons, and even fresh lavender. With a commitment to quality, Gelateria del Teatro creates a memorable experience for gelato lovers, offering both classic and unexpected flavors in a charming, tucked-away location.

Pompi – Il Regno del Tiramisù

For tiramisu lovers, Pompi is the go-to spot in Rome, often referred to as "The Kingdom of Tiramisu." With multiple locations around the city, Pompi is famous for its variety of tiramisu flavors, including classic, strawberry, and even pistachio versions. Their portions are generous, and each bite of their creamy, coffee-soaked delicacy is a dream. Whether you're grabbing a tiramisu on the go or enjoying it in the shop, Pompi is a sweet staple in Rome.
